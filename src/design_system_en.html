Product Design

Bridge design system

This project refers to the Bridge One® design system, a company management platform that encompasses ideation and task management functionalities.

Challenge

It was a process of modernizing the UI in conjunction with the search for consistency and visual unity between the screens. The main challenge was the inventory created based on what already existed both in terms of design and front end.

My role

The project involved a team with 2 designers and 4 front end developers. My role was to collect existing components for debate, participate in meetings and decision-making in addition to creating new components.

Process

The process began with an inventory of existing components and their architecture at different levels of atomization. To this end, some card sorting sessions were held, alternating individual and team moments for decision making.

After passing this inventory and architecture phase, the component design phase began, first with the simplest ones and gradually moving on to the more complex ones. Depending on the component, some sessions were held as a team to analyze alternatives and make operational decisions. Always aiming for clean code, based on material design, and interface standardization.

The result was a well-structured design system in Figma and paired with the front end. Which continouous to be updated and changed according to new discoveries.
